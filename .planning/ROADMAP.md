# Roadmap: AI Automation Agency

**Project:** AI Automation Agency — Solo Operator Serving Local Service Businesses
**Depth:** Quick (4 phases)
**Coverage:** 25/25 v1 requirements mapped
**Created:** 2026-02-27

---

## Phases

- [ ] **Phase 1: Brand & Foundation** - Agency has an identity and documented commercial terms before anything is built or sold
- [ ] **Phase 2: PoC Build** - Lead follow-up automation is live, documented, and filmed — producing the core sales asset
- [ ] **Phase 3: Sales Surface** - Landing page and productized packages are live; a prospect can find the agency and book a call
- [ ] **Phase 4: Acquisition Engine** - YouTube pivoted, first videos published, cold outreach running, and leads entering the pipeline

---

## Phase Details

### Phase 1: Brand & Foundation

**Goal**: The agency has a name, visual identity, and commercial terms — enough to show up professionally in any conversation
**Depends on**: Nothing (first phase)
**Requirements**: BRAND-01, BRAND-02
**Success Criteria** (what must be TRUE):

1. The agency has a name, registered domain, and a branded email address that sends and receives
2. A logo, color palette, and typography choice exist and are consistently applied
3. You can send a cold email or hand a business card that does not embarrass you
   **Plans**: TBD

---

### Phase 2: PoC Build

**Goal**: The lead follow-up automation is live, running for the agency's own inquiries, documented as a case study, and filmed as a walkthrough video
**Depends on**: Phase 1
**Requirements**: POC-01, POC-02, POC-03, POC-04
**Success Criteria** (what must be TRUE):

1. A new lead submitted via web form triggers AI qualification, appointment booking via Google Calendar, and sends a confirmation — end-to-end, without human intervention
2. The automation uses the defined stack: N8N + Claude/LLM + Twilio or Resend + Google Calendar
3. A written case study exists with specific before/after numbers (response time, leads captured, estimated revenue recovered)
4. A 3-5 minute screen-recorded walkthrough video of the PoC is complete and ready to embed or publish
   **Plans**: TBD

---

### Phase 3: Sales Surface

**Goal**: A prospect who finds the agency online can understand what it does, see a working demo, see published pricing, and book a discovery call — all in one place
**Depends on**: Phase 2
**Requirements**: LAND-01, LAND-02, LAND-03, LAND-04, LAND-05, LAND-06, PKG-01, PKG-02, PKG-03, PKG-04
**Success Criteria** (what must be TRUE):

1. The landing page is live and explains the value proposition in plain English a plumber or dentist would understand
2. Three to five productized automation packages are listed with fixed published prices in the $1,500-3,000 range
3. The landing page shows at least one concrete ROI example (e.g., "recover 8 lost leads per week") using real numbers from the PoC case study
4. The PoC case study or video is embedded on the page as social proof
5. A single primary CTA — book a discovery call — is functional and routes to Google Calendar booking
6. N8N templates exist for the listed packages (minimum 3 templates: Lead Follow-Up, Missed Call Text-Back, Review Request / Appointment Reminders)
   **Plans**: TBD

---

### Phase 4: Acquisition Engine

**Goal**: The agency is actively generating awareness and leads — YouTube channel pivoted and publishing, cold outreach running to a prospecting list, and at least one prospect in the pipeline
**Depends on**: Phase 3
**Requirements**: CONT-01, CONT-02, CONT-03, CONT-04, CONT-05, LEAD-01, LEAD-02, LEAD-03, LEAD-04
**Success Criteria** (what must be TRUE):

1. The YouTube channel has updated branding, a revised about section, and the first 5 videos published — all speaking to business owners, not builders
2. A prospecting list of local service businesses exists (Google Maps or Yelp sourced, target area)
3. A personalized cold outreach sequence is ready: email/DM templates for Day 1 intro, Day 3 follow-up, Day 7 value-add
4. A free audit offer is defined and deliverable: "I found 3 things costing you leads" personalized to a prospect's business type
5. At least one prospect has received outreach or booked a discovery call
