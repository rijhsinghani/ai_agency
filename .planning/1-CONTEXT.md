## Naming Decisions

| Decision         | Choice                                                     | Reasoning                                                                                                                    |
| ---------------- | ---------------------------------------------------------- | ---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Brand name       | **Sameer Automations**                                     | Personal brand. Solo operator where the founder IS the differentiator. "I build automations for small businesses."           |
| Domain           | **sameerautomations.com**                                  | Confirmed available (NXDOMAIN). .com is mandatory for SMB trust.                                                             |
| Entity structure | DBA under Raj Photo Video LLC                              | Not the focus — move fast, leverage existing legal structure                                                                 |
| "AI" in name     | **No**                                                     | Research: 51% trust gap, AI washing stigma, trades/SMB owners are skeptical. Use "AI" in taglines/copy, not the brand name.  |
| Scope            | **USA national**                                           | Not geographically limited                                                                                                   |
| Target market    | **Small businesses** (not just "local service businesses") | Broader framing. Trades (plumbers, HVAC, electricians) are the initial vertical, but the brand works for any small business. |

### Positioning Statement

**"I build automations for small businesses."**

- Solo operator + AI agents, not an agency with employees
- Lean into decade+ of product development and small business experience
- Sameer is the credibility — personal brand, not corporate facade
- Future vision: orchestrate AI agents to run entire small business operations

### Competitive Landscape (from research)

- Top competitors (Avoca, Hatch, Broadly, LeadTruffle) do NOT use "AI" in their names
- Successful agencies use taglines to explain AI/automation, not the brand name
- The trades/SMB vertical is being targeted but not yet owned by a personal brand
- "Done-for-you" framing is the single most powerful objection handler for non-tech audiences
- Small business owners search for specific problems ("missed call follow up"), never "AI automation agency"

### Words That Sell to SMB Owners

- More booked jobs, never miss a call, save 10 hours/week
- Done-for-you, works while you're on the job, set it and forget it
- Proven, field-tested, simple, straightforward

### Words to Avoid

- Revolutionary, disruptive, cutting edge, digital transformation
- AI-powered (in prominent positioning), synergy, innovative
- Partner (overused), unique (meaningless without specifics)

### Key Design Rules to Carry Forward

1. Logo minimum clear space = 1/2 of logo height on all sides
2. Minimum logo display size = 70px / 18.5cm
3. Scale logo proportionally (never stretch)
4. Black logo on light backgrounds, white logo on dark backgrounds
5. Full-color logo is primary; B&W for single-color contexts
