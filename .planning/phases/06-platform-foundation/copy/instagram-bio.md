# Instagram Bio Copy

**Platform specs:** 150 characters max. Supports line breaks. URL goes in the Website/Links field (separate from bio).

---

## Recommended Options

### Option 1 (PREFERRED)

**Copy:**

```
Losing jobs to missed calls and slow follow-up
I build the automations that fix that
Book a free 15-min discovery call below
```

**Character count:** 116 / 150

**Rationale:** Three clean lines. Each line is a distinct thought — problem, solution, action. Line breaks create visual rhythm and stop the scan on mobile. "Losing jobs" is the pain. "Fix that" is the solution. CTA points to the Website field ("below" references where the link sits). No emojis. Direct.

---

### Option 2

**Copy:**

```
Small business leads go cold in under 5 minutes
I build systems that respond in 60 seconds
Book a free 15-min discovery call below
```

**Character count:** 127 / 150

**Rationale:** More data-forward opener ("under 5 minutes" / "60 seconds" creates tension). Works for an audience that responds to contrast and specificity. Slightly more technical feel than Option 1.

---

### Option 3

**Copy:**

```
Missed calls are costing you jobs right now
I build automations that fix the back end
Book a free 15-min discovery call below
```

**Character count:** 116 / 150

**Rationale:** "Right now" creates urgency. "Fix the back end" speaks to the systems-minded business owner. Slightly more insider framing — good if the audience skews toward business owners who already understand they have a process problem.

Paste the landing page URL in the dedicated **Website** or **Links** field in Instagram Edit Profile. This is separate from the bio and does not count against the 150-character limit.

- Use line breaks between each line for visual chunking on mobile (Instagram supports manual line breaks in the bio field)

- CTA is "Book a free 15-min discovery call below" — "below" refers to the bio link field location on Instagram
- Pain signals drawn from topic-bank.md rank 4 (missed calls) and rank 9 (slow response times)
