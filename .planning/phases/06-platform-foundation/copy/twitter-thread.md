## Posting Instructions

1. Open Twitter, click "Compose" (pencil icon)
2. Write Tweet 1
3. Click the "+" button below Tweet 1 to add Tweet 2
4. Repeat through Tweet 5
5. Click "Post all" to publish the full thread
6. Go to your profile, find Tweet 1, click the three-dot menu, select "Pin to your profile"

The pinned tweet shows Tweet 1 at the top of your profile. Visitors tap to expand the full thread.
