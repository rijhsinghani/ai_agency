# Twitter Bio Copy

**Platform specs:** 160 characters max. URL goes in the Website field (not counted in bio).

---

## Recommended Options

### Option 1 (PREFERRED)

**Copy:**

> You're losing leads to voicemail while on a job. I build the automations that text back in 60 seconds. Book a free 15-min discovery call

**Character count:** 137 / 160

**Rationale:** Leads with the exact pain signal from rank 4 and rank 9 ("Missed 3 calls today while on a roof" + "leads going cold from slow response times"). "While on a job" is visceral and specific to the trade business audience. "Text back in 60 seconds" is a concrete result. CTA is the exact locked wording. No emojis.

---

### Option 2

**Copy:**

> Missed calls and slow follow-up are costing you jobs. I build automations that fix that. Book a free 15-min discovery call

**Character count:** 119 / 160

**Rationale:** Broader framing — covers both pain signals (missed calls + slow follow-up) without specifying a vertical. Slightly less visceral than Option 1 but clean and direct.

---

### Option 3

**Copy:**

> 40% of inbound leads go cold because no one followed up fast enough. I build systems that don't let that happen. Book a free 15-min discovery call

**Character count:** 148 / 160

**Rationale:** Leads with the research-backed stat ("Speed to lead is everything... closes 40% vs 10%"). More data-forward. Works well if you want a credibility-first hook before the CTA.

---

## Notes

- All options use contractions where natural and direct, peer-level tone
- No emojis per brand voice rules
- CTA is exactly "Book a free 15-min discovery call" (locked wording — no variation)
- Pain signals drawn from topic-bank.md: rank 4 (missed calls) and rank 9 (slow response times)
