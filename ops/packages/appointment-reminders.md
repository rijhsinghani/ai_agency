# Appointment Reminder Sequence

**Build Price:** $1,500
**Monthly Retainer:** $200/mo

---

## What This Does (Plain English)

A customer books a job for Thursday. Today is Monday. By Thursday, a meaningful percentage of them have forgotten, had a conflict arise, or simply won't be home when you show up. This automation sends a reminder 24 hours before, then again 1 hour before the appointment. No-shows drop significantly.

You stop wasting drive time on empty jobs. If they need to reschedule, they can do it right from the text — without calling you, without phone tag, without you getting pulled off another job to manage logistics.

## What's Included

- Integration with your booking system (Cal.com, Google Calendar, Jobber, Housecall Pro, or similar)
- 24-hour reminder via SMS and email with job details, appointment time, and a confirm/reschedule link
- 1-hour reminder via SMS: shorter, friendlier tone, with your phone number in case they need to reach you urgently
- Reschedule link routes directly to your booking page — customers can pick a new time themselves, no phone call required
- Cancellation handling: if a customer cancels, you receive an immediate notification and the time slot opens for rebooking
- Optional post-appointment follow-up text ("How did everything go?") — combines cleanly with the Review & Reputation package if you want both running together
- Monthly report showing reminders sent, confirmation rate, and cancellation rate so you can track improvement over time
- Handoff document and Loom walkthrough video

## Who This Is For

Any appointment-based local service business. Strongest fit for: HVAC maintenance and tune-ups, dentists, chiropractors, house cleaning services, pest control, and any trade doing scheduled (not emergency) work.

The trigger to buy is a no-show rate above 10%. If more than 1 in 10 appointments results in a wasted trip, this package pays for itself quickly. For businesses running 30-50 appointments per month, even cutting no-shows in half has a significant financial impact.

## ROI Example

A cleaning service runs 40 appointments per month. Their current no-show rate is 15%, meaning 6 appointments per month are wasted — the technician drives out and no one's home, or the customer cancels day-of with no time to fill the slot.

At $300 per cleaning job:

- 6 wasted jobs/month = **$1,800 in lost revenue + wasted labor time**
- After reminders, no-show rate drops to 7% (3 wasted jobs) = **3 recovered jobs = $900/month**
- Build fee of $1,500 pays back in **under 2 months**
- $200/mo retainer covers less than one average job per month

For higher-ticket services (HVAC maintenance, dental visits), the math improves faster.

## What You Need to Provide

Before work begins, you'll need to supply:

- **Booking system access:** login or API credentials for Cal.com, Google Calendar, Jobber, Housecall Pro, or whichever tool you use to manage appointments
- **Customer phone numbers in appointment records:** the system needs a phone number attached to each booking to send the reminder — most modern booking tools collect this at booking time
- **Customer email addresses:** for the 24-hour email reminder (optional if SMS-only is preferred)
- **Preferred reminder timing:** the standard is 24 hours and 1 hour before — let us know if you'd like different intervals
- **Reschedule policy:** how far in advance can a customer reschedule without it creating problems? This determines how the reschedule link is configured
- **Cancellation notification preference:** email, SMS, or both when a customer cancels
- **Twilio account:** for SMS sending — will be created and configured for you if needed (typically $5-15/mo in SMS costs at standard appointment volume)
- **Business name and technician name (optional):** to personalize the reminder messages
