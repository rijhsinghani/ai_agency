## What This Does (Plain English)

Two hours after you finish a job and the customer is still happy, they get a friendly text: "Thanks for choosing us today — if you had a great experience, it would mean a lot if you left us a quick Google review." One tap, the review page opens directly. Most satisfied customers will do it if you ask at the right moment.

Your star rating climbs. New customers trust you more. Google ranks you higher in local search. All automatic, every single job, without you lifting a finger.

## What's Included

- Trigger connected to your scheduling app (ServiceTitan, Housecall Pro, Jobber, Square Appointments, or a manual Google Sheet entry) — fires automatically when a job is marked complete
- 2-hour delay after job completion, timed to catch the peak satisfaction window before the customer moves on with their day
- Personalized SMS with the customer's first name and your business name — reads as a personal note, not a mass text
- Direct link to your Google Business Profile review page — one tap gets them there, no searching required
- Optional 3-day follow-up text if no review is posted — a gentle second ask for customers who meant to do it but forgot
- Review alert: you receive an email notification whenever a new Google review is posted, so you can respond promptly
- Monthly review count report so you can track progress over time
- Handoff document and Loom walkthrough video

Any small business that needs more Google reviews. Especially strong for: dental practices, salons, cleaning services, trades, law firms, gyms, photography studios, auto repair shops — businesses where Google reviews directly drive new customer decisions.

If your Google Business Profile has fewer than 50 reviews, or if your rating is below 4.5 stars, this package has an outsized impact. Reviews compound: each new review makes the next customer more likely to choose you, and Google's algorithm rewards profiles with steady review activity.

## What You Need to Provide

Before work begins, you'll need to supply:

- **Scheduling platform access:** login or API credentials for ServiceTitan, Housecall Pro, Jobber, Square Appointments, or your current booking tool — or a shared Google Sheet if you track jobs manually
- **Google Business Profile access:** admin access to your Google Business Profile (the account that manages your Google Maps listing) so the review link can be generated and notifications configured
- **Customer phone numbers in your job records:** the automation needs a phone number for each completed job to send the text — most scheduling apps store this automatically
- **Preferred sender name:** whose name should appear in the text? (Your name, your business name, or both)
- **Twilio account:** for SMS sending — will be created and configured for you if you don't have one (typically $5-10/mo in SMS costs at standard job volume)
- **Email address for review alerts:** where to send notifications when new reviews come in
