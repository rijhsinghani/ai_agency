# Missed Call Text-Back

**Build Price:** $1,500
**Monthly Retainer:** $200/mo

---

## What This Does (Plain English)

You're under a sink, on a roof, or driving between jobs. Your phone rings — you can't answer. Right now, that lead probably calls your competitor next. With this automation, the moment a call goes unanswered, they get a text: "Hey, caught your call — tied up on a job. Can I reach you in the next hour?" Most people reply. The lead stays warm until you're free.

No app. No extra step from you. It happens automatically, every time.

---

## What's Included

- Phone number setup (using Twilio, integrated with your existing business number or a new dedicated line) to detect missed calls in real time
- Automatic SMS sent within 30 seconds of a missed call — before the prospect even thinks about calling someone else
- Customizable message template: the text reads as if you wrote it yourself ("Hi, it's [Name] from [Business] — saw I missed your call...")
- Optional AI-powered reply handling: if the prospect responds with a question, the AI can answer basic inquiries and keep the conversation going until you're free
- Simple lead log capturing every missed call: caller number, time of call, whether they replied, and current status
- Log delivered to a Google Sheet, Notion database, or Airtable — your choice
- Testing with 20+ simulated missed call scenarios before go-live
- Twilio account setup and number configuration handled for you if needed
- Handoff document and Loom walkthrough video so you understand exactly what's running

## What You Need to Provide

Before work begins, you'll need to supply:

- **Your current business phone number:** to determine whether to use call forwarding or set up a new Twilio number alongside it
- **Twilio account:** one will be created and configured for you — you'll need to verify your identity (standard telecom requirement) and add a payment method for SMS usage (typically $5-15/mo in SMS costs depending on call volume)
- **Preferred message text:** a draft of how you'd want the text to read — or just describe your tone and it will be written for you
- **Response handling preference:** do you want the AI to handle follow-up replies, or just send the initial text and let you take over?
- **Lead log destination:** Google Sheet, Notion, Airtable, or another option — just pick one
- **Your name and business name:** to personalize the outgoing message
