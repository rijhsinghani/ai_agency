# Lead Nurture & Quote Follow-Up

**Build Price:** $2,000
**Monthly Retainer:** $300/mo

---

## What This Does (Plain English)

You give someone a quote. They say "let me think about it." Then nothing. Most businesses never follow up — or they mean to and forget. This automation sends a sequence of messages automatically: Day 2, a friendly check-in. Day 5, an offer to answer questions. Day 10, a gentle urgency note about the quote expiring.

All personalized. All sent automatically. You close the jobs your competitors forgot about.

The moment a prospect replies or books, the automation stops — no awkward messages after they've already said yes.

---

## What's Included

- Trigger connected to your estimating software or a simple Google Sheet or Airtable table — fires when a quote is sent or logged
- 3-touch follow-up sequence over 10 days, delivered via SMS and email:
  - Day 2: "Just checking in on the quote we sent for [job type]"
  - Day 5: "Any questions I can answer before you decide?"
  - Day 10: "The quote is set to expire soon — happy to lock in that price if you're ready"
- Personalized messages with your business name, the prospect's name, quote amount, and job type pulled from your records
- AI-written message variants for A/B testing — two slightly different versions of each message to see which converts better over time
- Stop trigger: the sequence pauses automatically if the prospect replies, books, or is manually marked as closed or dropped
- Lead status log in Notion or Airtable: Sent Quote > Followed Up > Closed or Dropped — so you always know where every open quote stands
- Monthly report: quotes sent, follow-up engagement rates, and close rate before vs. after
- Handoff document and Loom walkthrough video

## What You Need to Provide

Before work begins, you'll need to supply:

- **Quote or estimate source:** access to the tool where you track sent quotes — this could be an estimating platform (Jobber, Housecall Pro, ServiceTitan, Workiz), a CRM, or a Google Sheet or Airtable table that you already use to log quotes
- **Customer contact info in quote records:** phone number and email address for each prospect (most estimating tools collect this automatically)
- **Quote data fields:** to personalize messages, the system needs access to: prospect name, job type or description, quote amount, and quote date — confirm which of these are available in your records
- **Preferred tone:** professional and formal, or conversational and friendly? Sample messages will be drafted for your approval before go-live
- **Quote expiration policy:** do your quotes expire? If so, at what interval? This determines the Day 10 message copy
- **Stop conditions:** besides booking, are there other statuses (e.g., "lost," "competitor chosen") that should halt the sequence?
- **Twilio account:** for SMS sending — will be created and configured for you if needed
- **Lead log destination:** Notion or Airtable preferred — one will be set up for you if you don't have either
