# Editing Checklist

> Tool-agnostic. Every item describes the outcome you are achieving, not which menu to click.
> Work through sections in order. Do not skip ahead to polish until the structure is right.

---

## Assembly Cut

- [ ] Import all raw files (talking-head segments + screen recordings) into a single project or timeline
- [ ] Sync audio if recorded to a separate device — confirm lip sync is accurate on every clip
- [ ] Label clips by section: `hook-take-1`, `hook-take-2`, `demo-screen`, `results-segment`, `cta-segment`
- [ ] Rough assembly in section order — do not cut anything yet, just sequence the selected takes
- [ ] Watch the full rough cut once through without touching the timeline — identify what is missing or unclear

---

## Cut Review

- [ ] Remove all dead air, filler words ("um," "uh," "like"), and repeated sections — keep pacing tight
- [ ] Remove the "state the topic" clip from the beginning (this was for your reference only, not for the audience)
- [ ] Trim pauses between sections to 0.5-1 second — enough to breathe, not enough to lose momentum
- [ ] Color grade or apply consistent look to all talking-head clips — the viewer should not notice a change between cuts
- [ ] Screen recordings: match audio levels to talking-head segments — no jarring volume jump when switching to demo

---

## Captions and Subtitles

- [ ] Generate captions — auto-captions via editing software or upload to YouTube for auto-generation after export
- [ ] Review caption accuracy line by line — correct any misheard words, especially: "Sameer," "automation," any client names or tool names
- [ ] Confirm captions are in sync with audio — no lines that appear before or after the spoken word

---

## Thumbnail

- [ ] Thumbnail follows brand guidelines: dark background (#1A1A1A), Roc Grotesk Bold text, no more than 5 words visible
- [ ] If split-screen: left panel = the problem state (stressed visual, crossed-out item); right panel = the result (clean interface, specific number)
- [ ] Thumbnail does not duplicate the video title — it complements it, adds intrigue or the metric the title omits
- [ ] Export thumbnail as JPG at 1280x720 minimum, saved to `content/videos/edited/[video-slug]/thumbnail.jpg`

---

## Audio Review

- [ ] Normalize audio levels — consistent volume across all clips, no sections noticeably louder or quieter
- [ ] Check for background noise spikes during scene transitions — silence between clips should be neutral, not distracting
- [ ] Final listen with headphones — if any moment would make you reach for the skip button, cut it

---

## Final Check Before Export

- [ ] Two CTAs are placed: mid-video at approximately the 60% mark (captures viewers who will not reach the end) and end screen in the final 15 seconds
- [ ] End screen has a clickable CTA element (card or overlay) with the booking link: https://calendar.app.google/psycao3CrXjGnmk48
- [ ] Video duration matches the target range for this episode (Video 1: 8-10 minutes) — within 90 seconds of target
- [ ] Watch the first 30 seconds one final time — this window determines approximately 70% of audience retention; if the hook does not hold, fix it before exporting

---

## Export

- [ ] Export at 1080p minimum — H.264 MP4 format preferred for YouTube upload speed and compatibility
- [ ] File named: `[video-slug]-final.mp4`
- [ ] File saved to: `content/videos/edited/[video-slug]/`
- [ ] Confirm file size is reasonable — if unusually large, check export settings before uploading
