# Publish Checklist

> Complete this before uploading. Every field in the description template must be filled in.
> Scheduling last — do not schedule until everything above it is confirmed.

---

## Before Uploading

- [ ] Title written — under 60 characters, leads with the outcome (not "How I built..."), sentence case
- [ ] Description drafted using the template below — all `[brackets]` replaced with real content
- [ ] Chapter timestamps prepared — you will update these after the video finishes processing, but draft them now based on your rough cut timing
- [ ] Tags prepared — 10-15 tags pulled from the SEO keywords list in `content/youtube/content-plan.md`
- [ ] Thumbnail file ready — 1280x720 JPG saved to `content/videos/edited/[video-slug]/thumbnail.jpg`
- [ ] Category identified — "Science & Technology" or "Education"

---

## Description Template

Paste this into the YouTube description field. Replace every `[bracket]`. Do not remove the chapter header, CTA block, or hashtags.

```
[1-2 sentence problem statement — what the business owner is losing right now. Be specific: name the situation, not just "the problem."]

[1 sentence: what this video shows — the specific automation and the concrete result it produced.]

---

Chapters:
0:00 [Hook — the problem]
0:10 [Context — who this affects and why it matters]
0:30 [The automation / demo]
[X:XX] [The results — the numbers]
[X:XX] [What you should do next — the framework]
[X:XX] [Book a call]

#SmallBusiness #BusinessAutomation #SameerAutomates
```

---

## Uploading

- [ ] Upload the final MP4 from `content/videos/edited/[video-slug]/[video-slug]-final.mp4`
- [ ] Upload the custom thumbnail from `content/videos/edited/[video-slug]/thumbnail.jpg`
- [ ] Paste the title — confirm it is under 60 characters after pasting
- [ ] Paste the description — confirm all `[brackets]` are replaced before saving
- [ ] Add tags (10-15 from the keyword list)
- [ ] Set end screen elements: subscription button + one video card (link to the most relevant video on the channel)
- [ ] Add one info card at the mid-video CTA point (approximately 60% of video duration) — link to the booking page
- [ ] Set audience: Not made for kids

---

## Scheduling

- [ ] Update chapter timestamps in the description with actual timestamps from the processed video — YouTube shows these after processing completes
- [ ] Set publish date to next Thursday at 9:00 AM ET (per content calendar cadence in `research/content-calendar-draft.md`)
- [ ] Review the scheduled post in YouTube Studio preview mode before closing the tab — confirm title, thumbnail, and description all appear correctly

---

## Post-Publish (within 24 hours)

- [ ] Confirm the video is publicly visible and playable — open from an incognito tab
- [ ] Confirm the booking link in the description is clickable and goes to the correct page
- [ ] Share to Twitter — single tweet, lead with the result metric, include the video link
- [ ] Share to Instagram — single post or story with the key takeaway, booking link in bio updated if needed
- [ ] Reply to every comment within the first 24 hours — early engagement signals improve distribution
- [ ] Pin a comment asking a follow-up question: "What's the process in your business eating the most time right now?"
- [ ] Log the publish date and video URL in `research/content-calendar-draft.md`
