## Critical Decisions Made (Brand Pivot)

### Brand Voice — NOT YET DOCUMENTED TO FILE

The brand positioning shifted significantly during brainstorming:

**OLD positioning:** "AI automation agency for plumbers/contractors/dentists"
**NEW positioning:** "I'm a small business owner who automated the painful parts. Now I help other business owners do the same."

Key principles:

1. **Personal brand under Sameer's full name** — face forward, @SameerAutomates handle
2. **NOT niche-locked** — serves all small business owners, not just plumbers
3. **Origin story:** Automated his own wedding photography business
4. **Enterprise background exists** (BNY, US Treasury market, API sales) but NEVER mentioned by name — say "across industries" vaguely
5. **BNY must not know** — no employer names, no financial services specifics anywhere public
6. **Three doors open:** agency services now, broader services later, products/courses eventually
7. **Content pillars:** real automations, case studies with numbers, behind-the-scenes builds, what's worth automating
8. **Tone:** direct, peer-to-peer, confident. No emojis. No jargon. No "plumbers first" copy.
9. **Universal pain:** leads, operations, clients, finances — problems every small business owner has
