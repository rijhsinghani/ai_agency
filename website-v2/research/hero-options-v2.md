# Hero options v2: AI growth partner positioning

Research date: 2026-04-12
Positioning: Full-service AI growth partner (not single-automation shop)

Voice rules: brand-voice.md -- direct, peer-level, confident, practical, transparent. No emojis, no buzzwords, sentence case, numerals for metrics. Banned phrases checked against banned-phrases.json.

---

## Option 1: The growth team angle

**Headline:** Your AI-powered growth team is already built

**Subhead:** Brand, content, email, reviews, and lead follow-up -- running 24/7 while you focus on the work that actually needs you. I build the systems. You keep the customers.

**Why it works:** Positions Sameer Automations as a full team replacement, not a single tool. "Already built" implies speed and removes the "how long will this take" objection. The subhead lists all five service categories without feeling like a feature dump.

---

## Option 2: The transformation angle

**Headline:** From invisible online to fully booked in 90 days

**Subhead:** Most small businesses have no brand presence, no follow-up system, and no content strategy. I fix all three -- with AI systems that keep working after the build is done.

**Why it works:** "Invisible online" is a pain point every struggling small business owner recognizes immediately. "Fully booked" is the outcome they want. "90 days" adds specificity and urgency. The subhead acknowledges the full scope of the problem, not just one symptom.

---

## Option 3: The FOMO / competitive edge angle

**Headline:** The AI advantage your competitors do not have yet

**Subhead:** While they are still answering calls manually and posting once a month, your business runs automated lead capture, email sequences, content pipelines, and review collection. Same industry. Different century.

**Why it works:** Competitive pressure is the strongest motivator for small business owners who are on the fence. "Different century" is punchy without being a banned buzzword. The subhead paints a specific before/after contrast.

---

## Option 4: The systems-that-work angle

**Headline:** We build the systems that grow your business while you run it

**Subhead:** AI-powered branding, content, email, and lead follow-up -- designed for small businesses where the owner is too busy serving customers to chase new ones. Built by someone who had the same problem.

**Why it works:** "While you run it" acknowledges the core tension -- owners are physically busy with clients and cannot also do marketing. "Built by someone who had the same problem" activates the origin story and peer credibility. Broadest positioning of all five options.

---

## Option 5: The "handled" angle

**Headline:** Marketing, content, and follow-up -- handled

**Subhead:** You did not start a business to write emails and chase reviews. I build AI systems that handle the growth side so you can stay on the work side. One retainer. Every system you need.

**Why it works:** "Handled" is a single word that communicates relief. The subhead reframes the problem as a misallocation of the owner's time, which is less threatening than "your marketing is broken." "One retainer. Every system you need." is the pricing transparency the brand voice demands.

---

## Cycling headline variant (for any of the above)

Inspired by Lambda's FontSwap and Bland's typewriter pattern, the headline could cycle the industry word:

**Static:** "The AI advantage your [cycling word] competitors do not have yet"

**Cycling words:** restaurant / salon / law firm / dental practice / cleaning company / photography studio / gym / tutoring center

This reinforces the "any industry" positioning from the brand voice doc while adding the interactive element the design calls for.

---

## Recommended pairing

**Primary hero:** Option 3 (competitive edge) with cycling industry word
**Secondary test:** Option 2 (transformation) as an A/B variant

Rationale: Option 3 creates urgency and works with the futuristic dark theme. Option 2 leads with the strongest emotional pain point. Both are direct, specific, and avoid every banned phrase.
