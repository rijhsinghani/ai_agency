## Who this hits hardest

Plumbers, electricians, HVAC technicians, roofers, and landscapers who receive web form inquiries or inbound calls and lose leads simply because they could not reply fast enough. Best fit: businesses missing 5 or more leads per month not because the prospect said no, but because the prospect never heard back in time.

## What the system does

Every new lead — from your website contact form, a Google ad click, or a missed call — triggers an AI-powered text response within 60 seconds. The system asks 2 to 3 qualifying questions ("What's the job? When do you need it done? What area are you in?"), handles basic questions automatically, and books the prospect directly to your calendar. You see a new appointment. The system handled the conversation.
